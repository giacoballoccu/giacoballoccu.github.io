I'm a PhD Student in the Computer Science Department at [University of Cagliari, Cagliari, Italy](https://unica.it/unica/). I'm supervised and mentored by [Dr. Ludovico Boratto](https://unica.it/unica/page/en/ludovico_boratto), [Prof. Gianni Fenu](https://unica.it/unica/en/ateneo_s07_ss01.page?contentId=SHD30371) and [Dr. Mirko Marras](https://www.unica.it/unica/page/en/mirko_marras).

My research interest lay in Recommender Systems and end-user Explainability tecniques. I'm currently working on generating explanations based on Language Models outputs. Previously I mostly worked with Knowledge-Aware Recommender Systems and Path Reasoning methods. 

I'm teaching assistant of Algorithm and Data Structure in my University (from 2022) and I previously interned for 4 months at Amazon as Applied Scientist in the Targeting Science Ads team.
