I'm a PhD Student in the Computer Science Department at [University of Cagliari, Cagliari, Italy](https://unica.it/unica/). I'm fortunate to be supervised and mentored by [Dr. Ludovico Boratto](https://unica.it/unica/page/en/ludovico_boratto), [Prof. Gianni Fenu](https://unica.it/unica/en/ateneo_s07_ss01.page?contentId=SHD30371) and [Dr. Mirko Marras](https://www.unica.it/unica/page/en/mirko_marras).

My research interests lie in the interface of Machine Learning and Information Retrieval mainly in the context of Recommender Systems (RS): Explainable RS, Knowledge-Aware RS, Evaluation, Fairness.
