I'm a PhD Student in the Computer Science Department at [University of Cagliari, Cagliari, Italy](https://unica.it/unica/). I'm fortunate to be supervised and mentored by [Prof. Ludovico Boratto](), [Prof. Gianni Fenu]() and [Dr. Mirko Marras]().

My research interests lie in the interface of Machine Learning and Information Retrieval mainly in the context of Recommender Systems (RS): Explainable RS, Knowledge-Aware RS, Fairness-Aware RS and Evaluation.
